Chance The Rapper

Bio

Chance The Rapper is an American singer/songwriter from Chicago, Illinois. He is a rap artist that got his first big breakthrough with his mixtape "Acid Rap" in 2013-2014. He has since when on two release two albums called "Coloring Books" and "The Big Day". His first album "Coloring Books" was the first album to be released for free and win a grammy for album of the year. Chance is openly christian in his music that has followed more gospel and joyful tones as he has released his music. Chance has also donated millions to his hometown of Chicago to help try and stop the violence and give kids a safer community to grow up and express themselves in. Take a look at his website Chanceraps.com"
